6. Weight Verification and Seal Integrity Check

Tolerance Limit: 5%
Check that the weights of 10 units per filling are within acceptable range.
Then check 5 units per 30 minutes or 5 units per 100 units if applicable.

When capping / sealing, visually check the presence of corresponding amount of cannabis material in container / pouch.
Ensure capping is properly aligned and tightly placed and secured, or heat sealing is properly sealed and there is no air leakage.
